Most genetic disorders are recessive. The fact that Michelle and Keith don’t exhibit the disorder is further evidence that this trait is recessive. The fact that their daughter has the trait indicates that they are both carriers of the disease. Therefore they are both heterozygous, and the probability of another child inheriting the recessive (disease causing) allele from both of them is ½ X ½ = ¼.
